Why Your Donation Matters

Blood Donation

Whole blood donation is the most common type. It's used in trauma situations, surgeries, and for patients with chronic illnesses.

Fact: Every 2 seconds someone needs blood

Plasma Donation

Plasma is the liquid portion of blood. It's used to treat bleeding disorders, burns, and for patients with immune deficiencies.

Fact: Plasma can be donated every 28 days

Platelet Donation

Platelets help blood clot. They're crucial for cancer patients, those undergoing organ transplants, and surgery patients.

Fact: Platelets last only 5 days

Did You Know?

Only 3% of age-eligible people donate blood yearly. Yet 1 donation can save up to 3 lives. The blood donation process takes less than an hour, and your body replaces the fluid within 24 hours.
